A) Admission Procedure


Eligibility Condition

1.All the students have to fill the eligibility form at the first year level in order to be eligible to appear for the university examination.

2.Optional subjects once selected will not be changed.

3.F .Y . B. Com. , B. B. A. , B. B. A. (CA) and external students are eligible for admission to S.Y . B. Com , B. B. A. and B. B. A. (CA) as regular students on the condition that they have passed FY or have ATKT.

B) Counselling during Admission

During admission, faculty members are available to counsel the students and guide them to select specialization / optional subjects which will help them in their career.

C) Documents required for Admission and Eligibility

1.Statement of Marks of H.S.C / other examination considered equivalent by the Board.

2.School/ College Leaving Certificate.

3.Passing Certificate of H.S.C. or equivalent examination.

4.Migration Certificate (in case of students from outside state)

5.Certificate in the prescribed "Appendix A" of eligibility form of the Board (for candidates coming from outside India)

6.Caste Certificate / Validity / Non Creamy layer (as applicable)

7.Domicile Certificate
